From Canvas to Wearable Art: Limited Edition "Self-confidence" T-shirts

As a part-time artist, I’ve also ventured into t-shirt design, and I must say, I’m absolutely thrilled with how they turned out. My artwork and creative brand can now be part of your everyday style!

The design process was challenging yet incredibly interesting and inspiring, and I’m so satisfied with how the t-shirts came to life. This limited edition unisex
t-shirt is unique and exceptional, and I hope it quickly becomes recognizable and widely seen on city streets.

The artwork on the back of the shirt is a painting I created on canvas, and it’s also the first sold piece from my exhibition.

For any questions and orders, feel free to send me a direct message on my Instagram or Email

I hope you enjoy wearing this unique piece and wear it with pride!
