The Art of Becoming: My First Solo Exhibition

On March 6th, 2024, I held my first solo exhibition at Concept 37 in Skopje - a cozy, creative space that truly matched the emotion behind my work. The exhibition featured 21 art pieces, each created with a deeply personal narrative and emotional intensity. What truly amazed me was the overwhelming interest - a large portion of the artworks were sold, which for a debut show felt both surreal and affirming.
